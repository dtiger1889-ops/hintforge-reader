# Changelog

All notable, user-visible changes to the hintforge reader land here.

## Unreleased

Real changelog entries land on the first `corpus-core-version` bump or other publicly-visible behavior change. See the [builder's `docs/corpus-format.md`](https://github.com/dtiger1889-ops/hintforge/blob/main/docs/corpus-format.md) for the version-bump rule and the [reader's SKILL.md](.agents/skills/hintforge-reader/SKILL.md) for the `MIN_SUPPORTED_CORE` / `MAX_SUPPORTED_CORE` constants this reader speaks.

Persona iteration, prose edits, and other non-format-breaking work do not bump the version and do not require a changelog entry.
